# Project Summary

## Title
SpaceX IPO Market Impact Analysis

## Objective
Analyze how SpaceX compares with recent IPO peers and the largest IPOs in history, focusing on market impact, offering size, investor paper gains, and Elon Musk ownership-benefit scenarios.

## Key Business Questions
- How large is SpaceX compared with recent IPOs?
- How large is SpaceX compared with the biggest IPOs in history?
- How much paper value could IPO investors gain under a market-price scenario?
- How much paper value could Elon Musk gain through ownership appreciation?
- What makes SpaceX different from a normal IPO?

## Key Skills Demonstrated
- Business analytics
- Financial analytics
- Python scripting
- Data cleaning
- Scenario modeling
- Data visualization
- Executive dashboarding
- Data storytelling

This project is for portfolio and educational purposes only. It is not investment advice.
